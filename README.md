# Cookies.pbix
This cookies business Power BI project depicts connection between two tables through sales column; a pie graph showing count of product by location & another pie graph showing count of product  & sum of sales by salesperson by salesperson along with three slicers for product, location & salesperson; one bar graph showing sum of sales by location & product & another bar graph showing sum of sales by salesperson & product along with three slicers location, product & salesperson; one line graph showing count of product & sum of sales by location & another line graph showing count of location & count of salesperson by sales along with three slicers for product, location & salesperson.
